Quick start

Get from download to first card fast

1
Install the app Grab the latest stable build above and run the installer for your platform.
2
Open Culler and plug in a card You can use the free workflow immediately, or unlock Pro later from the license screen.
3
Activate when you are ready If you bought Pro, paste your activation code into Settings → License to enable the paid features.
Requirements

What you need

macOS

  • macOS 12 Monterey or later
  • Apple Silicon or Intel
  • About 80 MB of disk space

Windows

  • Windows 10 or 11, 64-bit
  • About 120 MB of disk space
  • Auto-updates via Squirrel

Both platforms

  • No internet needed after install
  • Works with SD, CF, and XQD cards
  • Camera USB-C and USB-A connections
  • FTP and FTPS output are optional

Good to know

  • Your photos stay on your machine
  • Free mode works without a credit card
  • Pro activation codes are emailed after purchase
  • Licenses can be renewed or upgraded later
